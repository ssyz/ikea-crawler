  • Ovenware – practical and effective

    Our ovenware is practical and stylish, coming in all sorts of shapes and sizes to cater to all your culinary needs. We know that preparing a meal for your family can be most enjoyable with the right ovenware, so be sure to get all you need to make life in the kitchen even easier. Our dishes come in a variety of different finishes, such as glass, ceramic and stainless steel. We’ve also got some non-stick options, allowing you to cook even the messiest of meals without worrying too much about the washing up later. You can even match some of our ovenware to some of the items in our tableware range.

  • Ovenware for different foods

    Preparing a meal doesn’t stop with ovenware; you can also find a whole range of items ideal for serving your culinary delights. Check out our SMARTA range, providing an affordable solution to serving and preparing by offering a number of duel oven/serving dishes, all of them are ceramic, and are dishwasher-safe. Our MIXTUR collection comes in glass designs that’ll look great in any modern kitchen, while our KONCIS roasting tins come in a couple of different sizes. We have every practical solution you need to help you create a delicious meal in your oven.
