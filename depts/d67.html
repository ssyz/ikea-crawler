Junior chairs

For a just-right height

Too big for a highchair and too short for a regular dining chair – what to do for that in-between stage? Our junior chairs give children the independence to easily seat themselves at an elevated height. These easy-to-clean, sturdy chairs fit with a variety of dining table styles, making them a welcome addition to any table.
