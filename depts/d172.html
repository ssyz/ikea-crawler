Ottomans

Space for more than just your feet

Coming in a choice of materials like fabric, leather and rattan, some of our footstools and pouffes are part of our different seating series, and others – like the new-entry with knitted covers SANDARED – are stand-alones. As well as that extra put-your-feet-up comfort, they make great seats for guests, too. Many also have storage space inside, so you can have things like magazines or throws close at hand but out of sight.
