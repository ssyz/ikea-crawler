  • Drawer units with a contemporary feel

    We have a range of drawer units in loads of different styles, and with plenty of different features and functions. They come in a number of materials, too; some are made of metal and others have a variety of wood finishes. This allows you to choose the material most appropriate to the décor and furnishings of your current office set-up. The same is true of the styles, which range from very contemporary to clean and simple line designs.

  • Drawer units with a variety of features

    Our drawer units can provide useful solutions to a range of storage issues in the home. Many of our units have castors, so you can move them around to where you need them – whenever you need them. They come in a range of heights; some are tall and freestanding, and others will fit snugly in place underneath your desktop. The MICKE range of drawers is at the same height as the MICKE desks, allowing you to extend your workspace. A number of different drawer sizes allow you to store a variety of different items, so you’ll find a place for everything, and everything in its place.
