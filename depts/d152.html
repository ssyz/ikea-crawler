  • Outdoor storage boxes to make the most of your garden space

    With our outdoor storage boxes you can keep your outdoor space looking neat and tidy. We believe that good design should extend both inside and out, so our storage benches are great for smaller spaces, as they double up as seating. Complete the benches with MUSKÖ storage bag to protect the contents, our storage benches can be used to store patio cushions, barbecue accessories, garden tools, firewood, children’s toys or even pet supplies. Some designs also feature castors, making them easy to move around.

  • Outdoor storage boxes: they’re benches first

    Visit your nearest IKEA store to see our range of outdoor storage solutions. Besides some practical and decorative shelving, we’ve got wall panels with hooks that can hold plant pots, and benches with handy built-in storage. They’re perfect for gardening tools, barbecue utensils, or a bucket and spade or a football or two. Create a cozy outdoor space, while making sure that it’s clutter-free as well. It’s easy with our range of outdoor storage furniture.
