  • Kitchen cleaning tools: complement your kitchen and keep it clean

    Our range of kitchen cleaning tools ensures your utensils can be washed thoroughly, dried easily and stored away neatly. Cleaning dishes is an essential chore, but it can be made fuss-free and quick with the right dish-washing accessories. Our designs are simple and sturdy; many are made of stainless steel, plastic or rubber. Some may even add a cheery hint of color to brighten your kitchen, so you can select the styles and tones that complement your décor best. We’re bound to have dish-washing tools to suit your kitchen design. So cheer up and roll up your sleeves.

  • Kitchen cleaning tools to keep your kitchen organized

    Washing and drying dishes and cutlery requires a variety of kitchen cleaning tools. Our PLASTIS dish-washing brush includes a small suction cup attached to the end of a handle, which sticks to your sink or ceramic tiles and keeps the area pleasantly uncluttered. Our stainless-steel ORDNING cutlery stand allows air to circulate to dry cutlery quickly. If you have a busy lifestyle, our ORDNING dish drainer allows you to leave washed plates and dishes to dry. Our lightweight KVOT dish drainer folds easily to save space, and is resistant to everyday wear-and-tear.
