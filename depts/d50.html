FOAM & LATEX MATTRESSES

Find the foam or latex mattress that’s right for you

Foam and latex mattresses mold to the contours of your body for personalized support and both materials absorb movement. Plus, these soft, flexible, resilient mattresses are long lasting because they can be turned regularly. Our foam and latex mattresses come with stretchable, knitted ticking on top that boosts point-elasticity, relieving stress on your hips, shoulders and neck.

Try it for 365 days. Enjoy it for 25 years
We’re sure you’ll love your new mattress. But just in case, you have 365 days to exchange it and try another. Once you’ve found the perfect one for you, we provide a 25 year limited warranty. Learn more about our 'Love it or exchange it' policy. Learn more about the 25-year limited warranty

State law requires all retailers to assess a recycling fee (plus applicable tax) on the purchase of each mattress, box spring and foundation to be delivered in the states of CT, CA and RI in order to support statewide recycling programs for discarded mattresses.
