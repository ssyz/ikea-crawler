Integrated lighting

Lighting that makes the details shine

Our integrated lighting is designed to work with furniture like bookcases, kitchen cabinets and wardrobes. It helps you light up the things you love to see, like pictures or mementos, or the things you need to find, like clothes or a favorite book. And by connecting it to one of our Smart Lighting solutions, you can even adjust the intensity of the light and set the mood for any daily activity.
