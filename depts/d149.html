  • Outdoor cushions to add color and comfort

    When Mother Nature is kind enough to grace us with sunny weather, you want to be prepared to make the most of it. There’s nothing better than relaxing in the garden or dining alfresco, but you’ll want to do it in comfort. Our collection of outdoor cushions offers designs that are suitable for lounging around on or to cover seats and backs. Try our coordinated HÅLLÖ series, or if you love patterns, check out our GRENÖ range in a variety of fabrics.

  • Outdoor cushions for any garden

    Match our cushions to our outdoor furniture to give your outside area a nicely coordinated look. We’ve got cushions that are custom-made for our outdoor dining chairs, and ones that make our lounge chairs even more comfortable to stretch back on. If you prefer a more subtle, neutral look, our ARHOLMA range of simple white outdoor cushions could be just what you’re after. With removable and washable covers and full care instructions supplied, your cushions will stay in tiptop shape. Your biggest challenge will be in deciding which color and style to buy.
