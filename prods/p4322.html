  • A cushion to cover every couch

    Our cushion range contains different sizes, shapes and colors, so we’re sure that we’ve got the perfect style for you to take home and nestle into the arm of your couch. A couple of cushions decorating the nooks and corners of sofas or armchairs add a nice finishing touch to the living room. Mix and match to find the right ones for your house’s décor. Try the traditional square GRANAT model or provide a quirky contrast to a divan with the IKEA STOCKHOLM RUTA, a bright tartan design of sunny yellow and hot pink.

  • A cushion that’s pleasant to plump

    A good cushion needs to be ready to undergo a fair amount of propping up tired feet, being squeezed under heads, or getting squashed behind backs. Our cushions put your comfort first; they’re made in soft materials, and are stuffed until they’re nicely puffy. Our KARLSTAD cushions are filled with duck feathers, making them squashy enough to shape themselves to your form. We’ve got different stuffing and materials to choose from, so you can rest assured that when you collapse on the sofa after a hard day’s work, our cushions are there to support you.
