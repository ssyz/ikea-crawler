-Feels crisp and cool against your skin as it’s made of cotton percale, densely woven from fine yarn.
-Made in 100% cotton - a natural and durable material.
-Fits mattresses with a thickness up to 18" since the fitted sheet has elastic edging.
Good to know
400 thread count.
Thread count indicates the number of threads per square inch of fabric. The higher the thread count the more densely woven the fabric.
Includes: 1 Twin flat sheet, 1 Twin fitted sheet and 1 pillowcase.
 
Care instructions

Machine wash very hot, normal cycle.
Do not bleach.
Tumble dry, medium, normal cycle.
Iron high.
Do not dry clean.
